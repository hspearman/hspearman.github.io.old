About Some things to know about me

Hi, I'm Hannah Spearman. I'm a Software Engineer in Chicago, Illinois. I like Web Development, but I like to learn everything I can about programming. I love programming so naturally I do it for a living-- even though it's my day job, I like to tinker with side projects as well.

When I'm not deep in the code, I jam on my guitar, play video games, bike, see concerts, draw, wish I had a dog, and constantly seek new adventures to go on. I like to keep a good balance of self-improvement and having fun. For me, they are often one-in-the-same.
